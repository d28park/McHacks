==========================
XTR3D VisualSkeletonSample
==========================

This sample shows the skeleton data on top of the acquired video.

Additionally, some other information is displayed:
* Camera status, engine status
* Tracking state (Initializing, Calibrating, Tracked, Not Tracked)
* Warnings: image-warnings (Strong backlighting, Too many people) and edge-warnings (Too far, Too near, Too left, Too right)
* Detected positions (Psi, Phi, hand up etc.), head position (0,1,2), gestures (Forehand, backhand, up, down), wings-position and position-combination.
* FPS (frames per second) of both the video and the skeleton.
* Reset button
